A dive into Vancouver's economy

Vancouver has a very upper-class economy, with huge industrial projects of luxury housing. The city is also known for its film making and is often referred to as “Hollywood North” according to InFocus Film School. (2025, January 13). Some major Block Buster productions such as X-men, Godzilla, and Deadpool are all Vancouver films. The Port of Vancouver is one of the country's biggest cargo-handling ports. Coal Harbour, which is in the same marina, is a very popular cruise ship station where massive cruise ships will pass underneath the Lions Gate Bridge.
