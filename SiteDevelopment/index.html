Welcome To Vancouver, BC

This is Vancouver, BC. Canada’s 3rd largest metropolitan, with a population of roughly 3 million people. It is situated in the furthest most southwestern point of mainland British Columbia. Vancouver is best known for diverse environment, from beautiful beaches and mountains to a multi-cultural city with a strong indigenous heritage. British Columbia is made up of about 75% mountains and Vancouver is a perfect place to explore the mountain ranges, go skiing, snowboarding and enjoying all four seasons. The city has had a growing population in the past few decades, with a large incline of forign immigrants.
