Biography

Crazy Dave is a character from Plants VS zombies. He is the owner of the house that you are trying to defend from the zombies. He also owns a shop where the player can buy perks and upgrades for their plants. Crazy Dave will usually appear before boss fights or special levels to inform the player about how the level will play out. You first 'unlock' crazy dave at the end of level 3-4. Shop

Index

Gatling Pea

The Gatling Pea is a reformed peashooter that fires 4 peas at a time, therefore quadrupiling the damage. The gatling pea can be purchased from Crazy Dave's shop for $5000 after beating atleast level 3-4.

Cattail

The Cattail is an aquatic plant that shoots spikes into zombies on the nearest row of lawn. This could be either on water or land. The Cattail is availble for purchase for $10000 and is unlocked after level 4-5.

Spikerock

Spikerock is a plant that injures zombies when they step on it. Something interesting about this plant is that zombies cannot eat it, however it does decay overtime from being stepped on. This plant costs $7500 in the shop and is available after completing levels 5-1.

Mushroom Garden

The Mushroom Garden is a alternate zen garden that is specifically designed for mushroom plants. This means the garden theme is night. The mushroom garden can be bought for $30000

Aquarium Garden

The Aquarium is a zen garden for water plants. The ecosystem of this garden is underwater. Similar to the mushroom garden, it can be purchased for $30000.

Gold Watering Can

The gold watering can is used for watering 4 plants at once in the zen garden. This is helpful to speed up the process of growing plants. It aswell can be purchased in the shop for $10000.
